Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-02-02

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our mobile application (“App”).

Important: The App includes an AI-powered chat. Messages you send in AI chat are transmitted to an AI provider to generate replies. Please do not share personal information (phone numbers, addresses, passwords, banking details) in AI chat.

1) What data we collect

Depending on which features you use, the App may process the following:

  • AI Chat Messages (User-generated content): text you type in AI chat and limited recent chat history needed to generate a reply.
  • App activity: basic interactions (e.g., which screen you opened) for analytics/performance.
  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID): used for showing ads and measuring ad performance (if ads are enabled).
  • Approximate location (optional): may be derived by advertising/analytics partners to show relevant ads and prevent fraud (this may be based on IP/region or device signals). We do not require precise GPS location unless the App requests it via Android permissions.
  • Diagnostics: crash logs and performance data (if analytics/crash reporting SDKs are enabled).

2) Camera, Microphone, Storage permissions

The App may request device permissions depending on features:

  • Camera/Microphone: for call simulation features if enabled in the App. We do not upload camera or microphone recordings to our servers unless explicitly stated in the App.
  • Photos/Media (Storage): only if the App includes features that let you pick images from your device. If your current App version does not include this, you should remove this line.
  • Location: only if the App explicitly requests location permission. If not requested, ads/analytics may still use approximate location derived from IP/region.

3) How we use data

  • Provide AI chat: send your AI chat message to an AI provider to generate a response.
  • App functionality: run call simulation and chat features.
  • Advertising: display ads and measure their performance (if ads are enabled).
  • Analytics & stability: understand app usage and fix crashes.
  • Safety: prevent abuse, enforce content rules, and handle user reports.

4) When we share data

We may share data with the following categories of partners:

  • AI providers to generate AI chat responses (your message and limited context are sent to the provider).
  • Advertising partners (e.g., Google AdMob) to display ads and measure performance.
  • Analytics / crash reporting (e.g., Firebase Analytics / Crashlytics) to monitor app performance.

Note: Partner data handling may be governed by their own policies.

5) Data retention

  • Chat history: stored locally on your device unless stated otherwise.
  • AI chat requests: transmitted to AI providers to generate replies. Providers may retain data for a limited time according to their policies.
  • Diagnostics/analytics: retained as needed to improve stability and prevent fraud.

6) Your choices

  • You can clear the App’s local data or uninstall the App to remove locally stored chat history.
  • You can manage permissions (Camera/Microphone/Location/Photos) in Android Settings.
  • You can limit ad personalization in your device settings (where supported).

7) Security

We use industry-standard measures such as encryption in transit (HTTPS) when communicating with partners and services.

8) Children’s privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
